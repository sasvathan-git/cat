Cats are common pets – Many people keep cats at home because they are friendly and easy to care for.

Cats are playful animals – They like to play with toys, balls, or chase small moving things.

Cats are very clean – They often lick their fur to keep themselves clean.

Cats drink milk and eat fish or cat food – These are some of their favorite foods.

Cats are good companions – They can make people happy and reduce loneliness
